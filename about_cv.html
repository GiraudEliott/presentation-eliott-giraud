Quick presentation

GIRAUD Eliott was born on the 27th of december 1998, around Grenoble. He's an ex-military installed in Grenoble, now studying Information Technology since 2021.

Passionate about music, design, informatique, he really is influenced by art. He also practices a lot of sports, principaly basketball, weightlifting and martial sports.

Drummer since he's 5 years old, he also plays bass and learns guitar. He produces electronic music, that you can listen on soundcloud, under the name of capkos.

He created his own clothing brand, OCCULT, in 2016, which has more than hundreds of sells. You can check his graphic designs on GEISTKRAFT. He even sells his own NFTs on opensea.

He is an autonomous and polyvalent learner, speaking fluent french and english. Planning on moving to asia in the next years to learn more about the culture and the languages, he's ambicious, creative, and a hard worker.

Visiting this website, you visit a small rift in Eliott's mind, showing you some of his music and graphic work.
